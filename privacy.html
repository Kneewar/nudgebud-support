NudgeBud Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

NudgeBud is built with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account or sign-in required
  • No app names or usage data ever leave your device
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No network requests of any kind

Screen Time Access

NudgeBud uses Apple's FamilyControls and DeviceActivity frameworks to monitor app usage locally on your device. This data is never transmitted anywhere and is only used to deliver the reminders and shields you configure.

App Group Storage

NudgeBud stores your Nudge rules and usage data in an on-device App Group shared between the app and its extensions. This data never leaves your device.

Purchases

NudgeBud Premium is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple. NudgeBud does not handle or store any payment information.
